ABOUT US

Sarvodaya Youth foundation was founded in the 21st century by the inspirational words of Martin Luther King "Darkness can't drive out darkness Only Light can do that Hate can't drive out hate,Only Love can do that." Today Sarvodaya Youth Foundation is amoung few trusted grass root NGOs of India, focusing on ending poverty, social injustce and also to vigilant the poor worker and former of our rural areas in different aspects of their day to day work, so that they all can not be cheated.

We do this through well-planned and comprehensive programs in health , education , livelihoods and disaster preparedness and response.We create social impact through Knowledge Management.

Our Vision

A society based on legitimate right equity, justice, honesty, social sensitivity and a culture of service in which all are self-reliant Sarvodaya Youth Foundation Focus And Sole Reason For Existance Is to Work as a Catalyst To bring Sustainable Change in the lives of Under Privileged Childern, youth and woman. The family of the child is the medium through which the development takes place.

Our Mision

To identify and work alongside the economically and socially deprived, so that they become educated skilled and aware.

Enable them to be self- reliant and enjoy a healthy ,dignified and Sustainable quality of life.

What We DO

We provide knowledge of computer, Internet, typewriting, shorthand, refrigiration, electrician etc. too have the opportunities of employment.We do this through well-planned and comprehensive programs in health , education , livelihoods and disaster preparedness and response.We create social impact through Knowledge Management.
